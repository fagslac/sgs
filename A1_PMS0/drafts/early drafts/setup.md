# AGI Version — Setup & Worldbuilding

*Alternate framing for the SGS serial. Proof of concept.*

---

## Premise

Tian is a postgrad in the Humanities Department (Reality Television division) at the unnamed university — the one that is **not** Fagslac / House of Wands. Classes are Content. The Content is a show.

The show: a posthuman reality TV time travel broadcast. Tian uses Chadt's brain as a signal relay to plug through the Imaginary Plane. The Imaginary = the broadcast studio. History = the set.

Chadt is the camera. Tian is the cameraman/floor manager. The audience is post-Singularity.

---

## The Four Houses

Four posthuman factions, each with a distinct lens for evaluating historical events. They compete in a live broadcast format — half medieval joust, half auction, half sports commentary, half academic roundtable.

| House | Suit | Lens | Specialty |
|-------|------|------|-----------|
| 🏰 House of Wands | Wands | Bytes | Information weight. How much data does this artifact encode? |
| ⚔️ House of Swords | Swords | Spacetime | Years × meters². How much reality-volume does this occupy? |
| 🏆 House of Cups | Cups | Lives | Capita. How many human lives does this touch? |
| 🪙 House of Pentacles | Pentacles | Money | USD log scale. Orders of magnitude of material value. |

Each house competes to estimate the **Value** of historical artifacts and events. There is no single correct answer — the debate is the point.

---

## The Format

### The Chair

Pentacles holds the gavel as default chair. The Ace of Pentacles runs the show Price-is-Right style:
- Presents the artifact
- Takes estimates from each house
- Audience decides the winner

### Phase 1 — The Price is Right / Antiques Roadshow

An artifact is placed on the table. Each house estimates its Value through their lens:
- "What's it worth?"
- Smaller, punchier interventions
- Brief back-and-forth
- Audience votes on who's closest

### Phase 2 — WWF Investigation / Detective Battle

After estimates, the show shifts gear. The houses become detectives:
- "Who created this Leak?"
- Arguments manifest as combat — word-becomes-magic
- Houses build cases, point fingers, clash
- The artifact responds: glows, shifts, reveals
- Cliffhanger: a name emerges

### The Sports Commentary / Pokémon Match Frame

When historical figures clash, the broadcast shifts to play-by-play:
- Arena manifests in the Imaginary
- Figures appear as avatars made of their historical moment
- Houses commentate like a fighting game match
- Abilities are named techniques (LitLitRPG — "twelve razor-sharp Principles")

### Word Becomes Magic

In the Imaginary broadcast studio, arguments have weight. If a house makes a strong enough case, reality responds — the artifact glows, the terrain shifts, the scale of the claim becomes visible. This is how the audience *sees* the Value without being told.

## Tone

- Lightweight, educational, easy to read
- Dodgeball / WWF / Terrace House energy
- The gravity sits in the **gap** between the fun format and what's actually being discussed
- No walls of text. Rapid back-and-forth. Interruptions allowed.
- Never "x is worth Y lives" — always "the scale here is approximately..."
